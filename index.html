A web hosting platform for artists and galleries

ArtCat is a web hosting service dedicated to helping artists and galleries build their presence on the web. We offer unlimited storage and bandwidth for your images, videos, and files, providing stress-free tools to get your site up and running quickly.

IMAGES

ArtCat will automatically generate thumbnails and other image sizes it needs to display them on your site.

VIDEO

Videos from YouTube, Vimeo, or other platforms can be embedded in your site, our we can host your uploaded videos.

THEMES

Our pre-built themes allow you to display your work without any knowledge of web design, HTML, or CSS. You may customize colors and fonts without any coding.

Features

  • Artworks organized into Portfolios for Artist Sites
  • Artists and Exhibitions for Art Gallery Sites
  • News items with option to display recent ones on home page
  • Press items as text, external link, image, or Word/PDF upload
  • Publications with description and cover image
  • Simple image galleries for non-artwork images
  • Free-form pages for about, contact, etc. including a contact form with spam protection
  • Integration with Google Analytics
